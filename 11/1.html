Login Modal

For user log-in flow, let's put a Log In button/link on your Navbar that opens up a authentication modal when clicked.

The modal should display a Login Form by default:

There should be a link to toggle the form into Sign Up Form:

You can have a showModal state handle the showing or hiding of the Modal.

You will also need another isLoginForm state to handle whether to show Login or Sign Up form.

Tips: Create 2 separate files/components for each Login and Signup form. Don't put everything together.
