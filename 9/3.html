Navigation Bar

It's time to have a proper Navbar for your app.

Create Navbar.js in /src/components:

src
├── components
|   ├── Navbar.js
|   └── Loader.js
├── containers
|   └── UserImages.js
├── pages
|   └── HomePage.js
├── App.js

Challenge

Move these <Link> component into <Navbar>:

<Link to="/">Home</Link>
<Link to="/user/1">My Profile</Link>

Style your Navbar, make it useful. Then include it in <App>.
